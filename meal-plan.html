Labor Day Grill-Out Caribbean ≤30 Yield: 8–10 plates + leftovers

Base Ingredients
  • Chicken breasts 4 lb (8 small fillets)
  • Lean beef patties 8 (¼ lb each)
  • Bell peppers 6, thick strips
  • Corn on the cob 8 ears
  • Watermelon 1, cut in wedges
Pantry Staples
  • Oil 3 Tbsp • Salt 2 tsp • Black pepper 1 tsp
  • Jerk blend 2 Tbsp (or 1 tsp each allspice, thyme, chili, +1 tsp brown sugar)
Steps (Beginner-Friendly)
  1. Preheat grill to medium-high (450°F). Clean & oil grates.
  2. Season chicken with 1 Tbsp oil + jerk blend + 1 tsp salt.
  3. Season patties with ½ tsp salt + pepper both sides.
  4. Toss peppers & corn with 2 Tbsp oil + ½ tsp salt.
  5. Grill chicken 5–6 min/side to 165°F; burgers 3–4 min/side; peppers/corn until charred-tender (8–10 min total, turn occasionally).
  6. Rest meats 5 min. Serve with watermelon.

Storage/Reheat: Cool within 2 hrs. Refrigerate 3–4 days. Reheat chicken/peppers in skillet 5–6 min; corn 2–3 min in boiling water or scrape kernels for bowls.

Approx/serving: ~480 kcal (burger + chicken + veg) • 35–45g protein • lower carbs if skipping bun.

Tomato-Lentil Stew over Rice Mediterranean ≤30 Yield: 8 servings

Base Ingredients
  • Red lentils 2 cups (rinsed)
  • Crushed tomatoes 28 oz
  • Diced tomatoes 14–15 oz
  • Onion 1 large, diced
  • Carrots 2, diced
  • Spinach 6 oz
  • Cooked rice for serving (~8 cups)
Pantry Staples
  • Olive oil 2 Tbsp
  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced
  • Cumin 2 tsp • Paprika 1 tsp
  • Broth or water 5 cups
  • Salt 1½ tsp • Pepper
Steps (6)
  1. Sauté onion + carrot in oil 4–5 min (medium heat).
  2. Add garlic 30 sec; stir in cumin & paprika.
  3. Add lentils + 5 cups broth; bring to boil, then simmer 12–15 min.
  4. Stir in crushed + diced tomatoes; simmer 5 min.
  5. Fold in spinach to wilt; season with salt/pepper.
  6. Serve over warm rice.

Storage/Reheat: 4 days chilled; freezes 2 months. Reheat on stovetop with splash of water.

Approx/serving: ~320 kcal • 18g protein • 54g carbs • 12g fiber • ~420 mg sodium (salted broth).

Eggplant–Tomato Pasta Mediterranean ≤30 Yield: 8 servings

Base Ingredients
  • Eggplant 2 med (about 2 lb), ½" dice
  • Crushed tomatoes 2 × 28 oz
  • Pasta 1½ lb (penne or rotini)
  • Spinach 6 oz
Pantry Staples
  • Olive oil 3 Tbsp
  • Garlic 4 cloves
  • Italian herbs 2 tsp
  • Salt 2 tsp • Pepper
Steps (4)
  1. Sauté eggplant in 2 Tbsp oil, 8–10 min till tender & browned; add garlic & herbs 30 sec.

Baked Fish Tacos + Lime Slaw Caribbean ≤30 Yield: 8–10 tacos

Base Ingredients

  • Cabbage ½ head, shredded
  • Lime 2
Pantry Staples
  • Oil 2 Tbsp
  • Chili powder 2 tsp • Cumin 1 tsp
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Optional: salsa
Steps (5)
  1. Heat oven 425°F. Sheet pan with parchment.
  2. Season fish with oil, chili, cumin, ½ tsp salt.
  3. Bake 10–12 min (flakes easily).

Storage/Reheat: Keep fish & slaw separate; reheat fish 3–4 min skillet; warm tortillas fresh.

Chicken & Broccoli Stir-Fry Mediterranean ≤25 Yield: 8 bowls

Base Ingredients
  • Chicken breast 3 lb, strips
  • Broccoli florets 2 lb (fresh or frozen)
Pantry Staples
  • Soy sauce ½ cup • Garlic 3 cloves • Ginger 1 tsp
  • Cornstarch 1 Tbsp + 2 Tbsp water (slurry)
  • Oil 2 Tbsp
Steps (5)
  1. Heat 1 Tbsp oil high; sear chicken 4–5 min; remove.
  2. Add 1 Tbsp oil; stir-fry broccoli 3–4 min.
  3. Return chicken; add soy, garlic, ginger.
  4. Stir in slurry; cook 1–2 min till glossy.
  5. Serve over rice/cauli-rice.

Storage/Reheat: 4 days; reheat covered skillet with splash water.

Pantry Staples

Stuffed Cabbage Rolls (Chili Swap) Mediterranean ≤30 Yield: 12 rolls

Base Ingredients
  • Green cabbage 1 head
  • Ground turkey or beef 2 lb (or lentils 2 cups cooked)
  • Cooked rice 2 cups
  • Tomato sauce 3 cups
Pantry Staples
  • Onion 1, diced • Garlic 3 cloves
  • Paprika 1 tsp • Salt 1½ tsp • Pepper
  • Oil 1 Tbsp
Steps (6)
  1. Boil large pot of water. Core cabbage; peel 12 large leaves; blanch 3–4 min till pliable.
  2. Mix filling: meat (or lentils), rice, onion/garlic, paprika, 1 tsp salt.
  3. Lay leaf flat, add ⅓ cup filling, tuck sides, roll up.
  4. Nest rolls seam-side down in oiled 9×13" pan.
  5. Pour tomato sauce over; sprinkle ½ tsp salt.
  6. Bake 375°F 25–30 min till cooked through.

Storage/Reheat: 4 days; reheat covered 350°F 10–15 min.

Chicken Enchilada Bake Mexican ≤30 Yield: 8–10 servings

Base Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas 20
  • Enchilada sauce 2 × 28 oz cans
  • Black beans 2 cans, rinsed
  • Cheese 8–12 oz (optional)
Pantry Staples
  • Onion 1, diced
  • Cumin 2 tsp • Chili powder 2 tsp
  • Oil 1 Tbsp • Salt
